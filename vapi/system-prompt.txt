Wait for response.

Step 4 — Convert the caller's date to YYYY-MM-DD format based

Wait for response.

Wait for response.

Wait for response.

Wait for confirmation.

Step 10 — Only after confirmation call book_appointment.
Then say: "You are all set! Is there anything else I can
